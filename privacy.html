隐私政策

最近更新：2026年6月25日。本政策向您说明在游玩黄金之路 LoL 时，本站（“本站”、“我们”）如何处理相关信息。

1. 概要 (Summary)

黄金之路 LoL 是一款无需注册账户、亦无服务端数据库的网页浏览器游戏。我们不会在服务器上要求、收集或存储个人数据。我们使用保护隐私的统计工具来了解网站的汇总使用情况，并展示可能使用 Cookie 的第三方广告。

2. 保存在您设备上的信息

核心游戏逻辑完全运行在您的浏览器中。我们不在服务端存储任何您的个人敏感数据。您的游戏进度、语言偏好和选秀历史等均保存在您的本地浏览器中（通过浏览器 localStorage 缓存）。

3. 网站流量统计

我们使用轻量级的网站流量分析工具（如 Vercel Web Analytics）来测量页面浏览量和网站汇总使用趋势。这些分析服务不使用 Cookie，且收集的数据均经过完全极简匿名化处理，仅作为我们提升网页访问体验的指标数据。

4. Cookie 与广告网络

我们可能会使用第三方广告（如 Google AdSense）来支持网站的日常运营成本。第三方服务商会使用 Cookie 根据您先前访问本站或其他网站的历史记录来为您定向展示更相关的广告。

  • 您可以随时通过访问 Google 广告设置 页面关闭个性化广告展示。
  • 您也可以访问 aboutads.info，选择拒绝第三方服务商使用 Cookie 投放个性化广告。

5. 您的选择 (GDPR / CCPA)

我们不出售任何个人数据。如果您希望清除本站产生的所有本地缓存数据，您只需随时清除浏览器的 Cookie 及本地存储（LocalStorage）即可。您在游玩过程中不会面临任何必须提交真实姓名或地理位置等敏感数据的要求。

6. 未成年人保护

本站不针对 13 岁以下的儿童，且我们不会在任何环节知情并主动收集来自未成年人的个人数据。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026. This policy explains how goldenroadlol.com (“the Site”, “we”) handles information when you play Golden Road LoL.

1. Summary

Golden Road LoL is a free browser game with no user accounts and no server-side database. We do not ask for, collect, or store personal information on our servers. We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate site usage, and we display third-party advertising, which may use cookies as described below.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The core game runs entirely in your browser. We do not use server-side storage for personal information. Your game state, language preference, and draft history are stored locally in your browser (via browser localStorage).

3. Analytics

We use lightweight web analytics tools (such as Vercel Web Analytics) to measure page views and aggregate usage trends. These analytics services do not use cookies and are designed to store fully anonymized metrics.

4. Cookies and Advertising

We use advertising networks (such as Google AdSense) to show third-party ads. Third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites on the Internet.

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

5. Your Choices (GDPR / CCPA compliant)

We do not sell personal information. Because we do not collect personal information on our servers, most requests can be fully satisfied by clearing your local browser storage and adjusting the ad-personalization settings linked above.

6. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
